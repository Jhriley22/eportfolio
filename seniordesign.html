Senior Design Project - ReLeaf

ME 421 & ME 424: August 2021 - May 2022

The purpose of the project is to create a toaster to better dry palm nuts (covered in chaff and mud), which are used to ultimately produce crude vegetable oil.

Problem Statement

Releaf is a Nigerian startup with a mission to industrialize food processing in Africa. They reached out to Duke to receive help with their palm nut drying processes
